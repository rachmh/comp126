About Us

Babe Haven is a high-energy queer punk band that riots in Durham, NC. With a mission to elevate femme-presenting individuals, people of color, and members of the LGBTQ+ community, they transform each show into a sanctuary of empowerment and acceptance. They released their sophomore record Nuisance earlier this year. Tracks like 'Blind Yourself' and 'Suck it Up' embody the unrelenting aggression and vulnerability that define their music. Babe Haven's music epitomizes not only femme rage, but the kind of catharsis that comes from finally being able to bark, growl, and let it all out without fear of judgment.

Babe Haven has quickly become a DIY staple of the Southeast. The members include Naomi Poesel (guitar/vocals), Lillie Della Penna (lead vocals), Brendan Martini (drums) and Julia Lynn (bass). Babe Haven has multiple releases under their belt such as their self titled EP, Babe Haven (2021) and two full-length albums Uppercut (2023) and Nuisance (2024). They mix high-energy punk with influences from nu-metal and riot grrrl movements, creating an inclusive and empowering atmosphere at their shows.
